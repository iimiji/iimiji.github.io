Mossy

Mossy is an AI-powered journaling application designed to support emotional reflection and well-being. It enables users to log their daily thoughts, analyze emotions using AI, and visualize mood trends over time.

Tools: React Native, Firebase, AI Emotion Analysis
Solo Graduation Project | SFU | IAT-499

View Prototype

Background & Problem Statement

Many people struggle with understanding and expressing their emotions, which can impact mental well-being. Traditional journaling can be helpful, but it often lacks immediate feedback. Mossy aims to bridge this gap using AI-driven emotion analysis and interactive journaling experiences.

User Experience & Interaction

The app allows users to enter journal entries, receive AI-generated insights on their mood, and track long-term emotional trends. Users can also visualize emotions through an interactive emotion wheel.

Technology Stack

  • React Native for cross-platform development
  • Firebase for backend storage
  • AI-powered sentiment analysis for emotion detection
  • Valence-Arousal Model for mood classification

Challenges & Solutions

One of the key challenges was ensuring accurate AI emotion analysis. To improve accuracy, the app integrates machine learning models trained on a diverse dataset of emotional expressions.

Future Plans

Moving forward, I plan to refine the AI model, conduct further user studies, and prepare a research paper submission to DIS 2026.
